Purpose

The goal of the Zarahemla Chamber of Commerce is to help to further the interests of small businesses in the community. The activities organized by the Chamber can range from hosting events, to lobbying local representatives to charitable works for the betterment of the community. This web site will provide more information about the events and activities hosted by the Chamber, it will also become a good source of training and updated information to local enterpenuers
