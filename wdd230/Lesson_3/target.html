Target Audience Descripton

These are the target audience of the Zarahemla Chamber of Commerce web site:

Any enterpenuer who wants to do business in our comunity, business men and women who want to imrove the economic sector in the area by creating a source of income, employies, and goods fo the people of Zarahemla. People who want to start a business but do not know where to start, people who want to make their business grow and do not how.
